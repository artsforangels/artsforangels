Signature Programs
How we make
it happen
Four programs, one heart — bringing art and community together in the most meaningful ways we know.
Community

The Story Quilt Project

Seniors and youth share their stories through art — creating collaborative quilts and murals that are donated to hospitals and community centers.

Intergenerational

Art by Mail

Young artists create illustrated letters and painted postcards for seniors in care facilities and hospital patients. Pen pals, but make it art.

Flagship Event

Annual Charity Auction

Local artists, community members, and workshop participants contribute pieces to our yearly art auction. 100% of proceeds support our partner organizations.

Outreach

Community Mural Project

We partner with shelters, schools, and hospitals to co-create murals with the communities we serve — art that lives in the neighborhood, forever.

Our Story

We believe art
belongs to everyone

Art Angels was founded in Wayne, Pennsylvania with one simple belief: that creativity is a basic human need — and too many people in our community are cut off from it.

Who We Are

Art Angels is a nonprofit organization dedicated to bringing our community together through arts and crafts — with a special focus on youth, seniors, and underrepresented communities who rarely get access to meaningful creative experiences.

We run workshops, host fundraisers, and donate art supplies, handmade goods, and artwork to local shelters, hospitals, and charitable organizations.

We're just getting started — and we'd love for you to be part of it.

Our Values

  • 🎨 Art is for everyone, always
  • 🤝 Community over competition
  • 💛 Generosity as a creative act
  • 🌱 Growth through collaboration
  • ✨ Joy is serious work
